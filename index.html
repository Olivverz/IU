I'm Lee Ji Eun

A Renowned K-POP Star

Hello!

I am a South Korean singer-songwriter and actress. The name IU is a combination of 'I' and 'you' meaning "you and I become one through music".

Career

MUSIC & ALBUM

I have released five studio albums, nine extended plays (EPs), 47 singles (including 19 as featured artist), five single albums, two remake albums and two compilation albums. I wrote more than 50 songs as well.

MOVIE AND SHOW

I acted in 5 films, 10 television series, 1 web series and 3 television shows. TV series called "Hotel Del Luna" starred by IU is the most popular K-drama in 2020.

AWARD & ACHIEVEMENT

Since debut in 2008, I have received over 200 nominations, winning over 100 of the nominations and won thirteen Melon Music Awards, twelve Gaon Chart Music Awards, five Korean Music Awards, eight Mnet Asian Music Awards, five Seoul Music Awards, and five Golden Disc Awards.
